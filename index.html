PyleaMUN 2026 Committees Agenda

UN Environment Programme (UNEP)

Balancing Ecotourism and Conservation in UNESCO Heritage Sites – Designing sustainable tourism models that preserve cultural landmarks while fueling local economies.

Security Council

Combatting Illicit Small Arms Trade – Developing international frameworks for awareness campaigns, policy proposals, and community outreach to curb the flow of illegal firearms and light weapons.

Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)

Sustainable Entrepreneurship: Youth in the Green Economy – Proposing incentives and capacity-building for student-led start-ups in renewable energy, circular design, and low-carbon tech.

UNESCO

Bridging the Digital Divide in Education – Ensuring equitable access to e-learning and digital tools for students in underserved urban and rural areas.

NATO

Reassessing NATO's Strategic Autonomy Amid U.S. Unpredictability – Debating the need for European strategic autonomy and defense integration in light of fluctuating U.S. commitments and rhetoric questioning NATO's relevance.

World Health Organisation (WHO)

Adolescent Mental Health: A Global Crisis – Formulating youth-centered strategies for schools and online platforms to provide early support and destigmatize care.

Human Rights Council (HRC)

Protecting the Rights of Children and Adolescents in Gaza – Crafting resolutions to ensure that youth in Gaza have access to education, healthcare, and protection from violence despite ongoing hostilities and restrictions on movement.

UN Women

Preventing Gender-Based Violence – Establishing international guidelines for safe reporting mechanisms and prevention education.

Historical Crisis Committee
